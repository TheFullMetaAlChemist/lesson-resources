Cathode rulePositive ions gain electrons. In aqueous solutions, compare the metal with hydrogen.
Anode ruleNegative ions lose electrons. Halides form halogens; otherwise oxygen forms.
Redox ruleOxidation is loss of electrons. Reduction is gain of electrons.
