2.4 Nanoparticles

Bulk & Surface Properties • Sizes of particles & their properties — detailed model answers, mark breakdowns, explanations, common mistakes and exam tips.

Calculation focus: cube volume, surface area and surface area:volume ratios
⚛ Key definition

Nanoparticles are particles between 1 nm and 100 nm. Their properties can be different from the same material in bulk form.

▱ Core property

They have a large surface area to volume ratio, so many atoms are exposed at the surface.

⚗ Common uses

Catalysts, sun creams, antibacterial coatings, electronics and drug delivery are common exam examples.
