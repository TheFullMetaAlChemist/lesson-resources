01.1 Name for safe drinking water

Answer: Potable water.

Reasoning: Pure water is 100% H₂O. Potable water contains dissolved salts but is safe for human consumption.

01.2 Making drinking water from fresh water

Answer: Sterilisation AND Filtration.

Reasoning: Filtration removes solid objects/dirt. Sterilisation (using chlorine, ozone, or UV) kills microbes.

01.3 Fluoride Percentage Calculation

\(\text{Percentage} = \frac{1.0}{4.0} \times 100 = 25\%\)
Reasoning: Divide the amount in the sample (1.0) by the recommended total (4.0) to find the fraction, then multiply by 100.

02.1 Resource Types

Metal is a finite resource.

Wood is a renewable resource.

Reasoning: Finite resources are used up faster than they form. Renewable resources can be replaced at the rate they are used.

02.2 How to recycle copper

Answer: Melting and recasting (or reforming) into different products.

02.3 Advantages/Disadvantages of Recycling

Advantage: Reduces waste / saves energy / conserves limited resources.

Disadvantage: Pan must be separated into different materials / expensive / energy intensive to collect and transport.

02.4 Energy Saving Calculation

1. Calculation: \(2,450,000 \times 0.85 = 2,082,500\)

2. Round to 2 Sig Figs: \(2,100,000\)

3. Standard Form: \(2.1 \times 10^6 \text{ J}\)

03.1 Agricultural waste removal

Answer: Organic matter AND Harmful microbes.

03.2 Screening

Answer: Removal of (large) solid objects.

Reasoning: This is the first physical step to prevent damage to machinery.

03.3 Products of sedimentation

Answer: Sewage sludge and Effluent.

03.4 Anaerobic digestion conditions

Answer: Without oxygen AND using microorganisms (bacteria).

03.5 Formula and State Symbol

\(O_2(g)\)

Reasoning: Oxygen is required for aerobic treatment. '2' must be a subscript.

04.1 Name of equipment X

Answer: Watch glass (or evaporating basin).

04.2 Independent Variable

Answer: The water samples.

Reasoning: The independent variable is what is changed by the investigator.

04.3 Mean Calculation

Answer: 0.13 g

\(\text{Mean} = \frac{0.12 + 0.14}{2} = 0.13 \text{ g}\)
Reasoning: 0.34 g is an anomaly and must be excluded from the calculation.

04.4 Prediction for Pure Water

Answer: 0 g (No solid left behind).

Reasoning: Pure water contains no dissolved solids. Therefore, when evaporated, there is nothing left to form a residue.
