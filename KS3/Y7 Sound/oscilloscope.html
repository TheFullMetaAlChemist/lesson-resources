Key relationships

  • Pitch is linked to frequency.
  • Volume is linked to amplitude.
  • Wavelength is the distance between matching points on two waves.
  • If wave speed stays the same, frequency and wavelength change in opposite directions.

Good prompts for students

  • What happens to the spacing of the wave when the pitch goes up?
  • What changes on the screen when the sound gets louder?
  • Can a wave be loud and low pitch at the same time?
  • Which parts of the wave would you measure to find amplitude and wavelength?
