Year 7 Light

Explore how light travels, reflects, refracts, forms images and creates colour

Topic Overview

In this topic you will learn how light travels, how mirrors reflect light, how refraction changes the direction of light, how the eye and camera form images, and how colour is produced by light, filters and objects.
