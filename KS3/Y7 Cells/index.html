B1 Cells

Exploring cells as the building blocks of life

Course Overview

In this topic you will learn how to observe cells, compare animal and plant cells, understand specialised cells and how substances move into and out of cells, and explore unicellular organisms. Work through the lessons below in order.

  • 1 B1.1 Observing Cells
  • 2 B1.2 Animal and Plant Cells
  • 3 B1.3 Specialised Cells
  • 4 B1.3 Apply Specialised Cells
  • 5 B1.4 Movement of Substances
  • 6 B1.5 Unicellular Organisms
